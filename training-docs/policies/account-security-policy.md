# Account and Security Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Document ID:** POL-ACC-005

## 1. Account Creation and Data

- You may create an account using your email address and a password, or via single sign-on (e.g., Google, Apple) where available.
- We store only the data necessary to process orders, provide support, and improve your experience. Our Privacy Policy describes what we collect and how we use it.

## 2. Password Requirements

- Passwords must be at least **8 characters** and include at least one letter and one number. We recommend a strong, unique password and do not allow commonly used or compromised passwords.
- You are responsible for keeping your password confidential. We will never ask for your password via email or phone.

## 3. Unauthorized Access

- If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, change your password immediately from "Account Settings" and review your order history.
- Contact support if you see orders you did not place or if you are locked out. We may verify your identity before making changes.

## 4. Account Closure

- You may close your account at any time from Account Settings or by contacting support. Closure is permanent; we will anonymize or delete your data in accordance with our Privacy Policy and retention rules.
- Open orders or pending refunds must be resolved before account closure can be completed.

## 5. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

- 2FA is optional but recommended. When enabled, you will be prompted for a code from your authenticator app or SMS when signing in from a new device. Recovery codes are provided at setup; store them securely.
