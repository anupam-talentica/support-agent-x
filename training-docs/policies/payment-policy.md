# Payment and Billing Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Document ID:** POL-PAY-003

## 1. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

- **Credit and debit cards:** Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- **Digital wallets:** Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal
- **Store credit and gift cards:** Applied at checkout when you have a balance

All card payments are processed through PCI-compliant payment gateways. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.

## 2. When You Are Charged

- For immediate-shipment orders, your payment method is charged when you place the order.
- For pre-orders or back-ordered items, you may be charged at the time of order or when the item ships, as indicated on the product page.
- Split shipments: If your order is shipped in multiple packages, you are charged once for the full order at the time of purchase. We do not charge per shipment.

## 3. Failed Payments

- If your payment fails (e.g., insufficient funds, card declined), your order will not be placed. You will see an error message at checkout.
- We do not retry failed payments automatically. Please update your payment method and place the order again, or choose a different payment method.

- For PayPal, the refund is credited to your PayPal balance.

## 5. Price and Currency

- All prices are displayed in the currency of the store you are shopping from (e.g., USD). Prices include applicable sales tax unless otherwise stated at checkout.
- We reserve the right to correct pricing errors. If an error is discovered after your order is placed, we will notify you and offer to confirm at the correct price or cancel with a full refund.
